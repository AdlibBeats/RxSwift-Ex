What are Baby Tokens (BBT) ?

Baby Tokens (BBT) - a valid cryptocurrency that you can earn and save to spend later on purchases. Our users can get rewards by accumulating Baby Tokens. You can earn Baby Tokens and exchange them for various services, goods or even money. BBT is the abbreviation of Baby Tokens. Baby Tokens are not a virtual currency limited to our application, it is valid cryptocurrency that uses blockchain technology. The amount of Baby Tokens available is limited, their value is determined by the crypto-exchange.

What is the Wallet of Baby Tokens ?

Each registered user of Pregnancy Tracker has a "Wallet" to store their Baby Tokens, and this wallet can be the subject of rewards. Through your wallet you can access your balance as well as the current tokens value expressed in USD. The wallet also displays a value through a pricing chart of 1 token in addition to a list of your transactions.

How to earn Baby Tokens ?

Only registered users are eligible to earn Baby Tokens. These are the actions that are rewarded with BBT: Daily login to the application. Every day, when you open the application, you will receive an reward. The more consistent days in a row you open the application, the more rewards you will receive. Go to the app every day to earn Baby Tokens! Reward for useful comments on articles and news. You can comment on the articles and give likes to the comments you like. If your comment receives enough likes, you will earn Baby Tokens! You can receive several rewards on the same article via more than one comment - the more active, the more rewards! In future updates of the application you will have more opportunities to earn Baby Tokens!
